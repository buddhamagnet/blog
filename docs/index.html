Into the woods

As I'm about to embark on a two year journey into the woods and beyond with the marvellous people at Woodland Ways, I thought I'd kick off a blog to document the sights and sounds of that mission. And any mountains I visit in the meantime. Or countries. And talk about some of the kit I use. With a side order of tech and radical politics and music.

About

My name is Dave Goodchild. I'm a writer, adventurer and technologist equally interested in the wilds and wide open spaces of the world and the fast-moving technology that is disrupting our culture. I believe that technology can be harnessed to make a difference.
